What Makes Onward Global Unique?

Developing business through personal introductions and word of mouth, Onward Global exclusively represents its clients and does not represent vendors or other supply-chain participants. Onward Global is YOUR man on the ground! This ensures alignment and effective representation while engaging your team, suppliers, and distribution partners. Services, deliverables and terms are clearly defined prior to each new engagement to ensure prioritization, transparency, and a result giving you full control of our achievements upon completion.

Examples of Client Engagements
Ready to start new product development and in need of a qualified vendor?
Onward Global will work with your team to understand your products and objectives and then identify 3-5 prospective suppliers capable of meeting your costing, R&D, quality and capacity needs.
Already manufacturing in China/Asia and curious if your supply-chain is operating at maximum efficiency?
Onward Global can audit your suppliers, systems and existing China/Asia team, helping evaluate strengths & weaknesses and proposing ways to increase transparency and optimization.

We can help you to scale and oversee vendors, staff recruitment, production, quality assurance, and more. Consider Onward Global your partner to work with your team to strategize, develop and execute.
